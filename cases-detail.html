Lineage II 15 years: Hot calendar

  • Special Project
  • Influencer Campaign

Our client, Lineage II, wanted to do something special for their 15th anniversary. We are proud to unveil a unique project that is bound to captivate everyone: the Lineage II Hot Calendar, featuring mesmerizing cosplayers, celebrates the game’s rich legacy in a visually stunning manner.

  • In December 2023, Lineage 2 celebrated its 15th anniversary. Our team’s goal was to organize a large-scale and memorable special project to honor this milestone for the game. Besides its music and stunning landscapes, the game is renowned for its characters. Consequently, we decided to create a calendar with 12 unique spreads, each featuring a character styled in the Lineage 2 aesthetic.

  • The AAA Agency’s team crafted the concept for each calendar page, set design and artwork. We collaborated with talented cosplayers, who flawlessly embodied the essence of the Lineage II characters. We developed the design and formulated the layout of the printed version of the calendar. Finally, the calendar was brought to life in a huge print run, ready to be flipped through and enjoyed by all.

  • Imagine 12 unique monthly pages, each presenting stunning photos of talented cosplayers embodying the game characters: elves and other magical beings. Each page immerses you in the world of Lineage II, with various game attributes and Easter eggs brought to life.

summary

We are incredibly proud of the calendar filled with references to the game. Each page features complete character cosplays from the game, along with thematic elements and accessories like paintings, figurines, logos, and more. We are confident that any fan of the game would be thrilled to receive such a gift.
